Services

Landscape Consultation

Professional landscape consultation by expert architects and horticulturists, offering expert guidance to transform your outdoor spaces with sustainable and aesthetic landscaping solutions.

Terrace Garden

Designing and creating lush terrace gardens that maximize green space, enhance air quality, and bring nature closer to urban living.

Butterfly Garden

Creating vibrant butterfly gardens that attract and nurture pollinators by incorporating native plants, nectar sources, and natural habitats, enhancing biodiversity and ecological balance.

Developing dense, fast-growing Miyawaki forests using native plant species to restore biodiversity, improve air quality, and create a self-sustaining green ecosystem.

Providing expert landscape maintenance services to ensure healthy, thriving, and well-manicured outdoor spaces through regular care, pruning, and sustainable practices.
