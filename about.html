Green Realm Landscape

Green Realm Landscape in Thrissur, Kochi, and Bangalore is one of Kerala’s foremost landscaping and maintenance companies.

Formed through the merger of Garden City Gardens, founded by Mr. Sabu Mathew in 2009, and Green Realm Landscape, founded by Ar. Sibin M. Sabu in 2015, the companies have been working collaboratively since 2017 onwards.

The firm is driven by client satisfaction and quality service, supported by a team of experienced, young, energetic, and passionate professionals. The team consists of Landscape Architects, Landscape Engineers, Botanists, and skilled landscape workers.

For the past 15 years, the company has maintained a strong commitment to excellence, integrity, and service.

Our projects include landscapes for residences, resorts, children’s parks, schools, and hospitality spaces. Our services cover Design, Construction, and Maintenance of Landscape, Hardscape, Mixed Farms, Horticulture Consultancy, Irrigation Hydraulic Design, and Installations.
